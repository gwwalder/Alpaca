JAPARI CAFE - EST. 2017

Created in 2017, Japari Cafe is renowned for being japari Park's first, and only cafe.
It's founder, Alpaca-Suri, believed quality Tea is what Brings friends
of all kind together. Using only locally sourced tea leafs and Coffee beans,
Japari Cafe maintains a high standard for quality, and a strong presence in the local community.

Japari Cafe serves a wide variety of
locally grown tea and coffee.


From as far away as the Savannah Region,
or as close to home as the Mountain Region,
Our menu is always Changing!
Choose from a variety of
pastries or gourmet Japari Buns.

is dedicated
